More about Daughters of Zion International

DOZI is an assembly of God’s daughters, Christ’s bride and the redeemed. Here, tribe, location, or class is no barrier. We are knitted together by Christ’s love.

We’re a non-denominational Christian ministry borne out in response to God's voice, inspiration of the HOLY SPIRIT and passion for the female generation with the burden to see ladies fired up, awakened to purpose and destiny, live up to their rightful position in CHRIST JESUS and be the best God has ordained them to be. DOZI is a place of equipping, building and training destinies; reconciling daughters (young and old alike) back to their heritage in God.

Meet the Convener

My name is Faithtestimony Akenroye. I'm a daughter of Zion, first child in a family of six, raised in a Christian home; committed to raising women of relevance after God's heart, seeing female folks fired up, live up to her rightful place in Christ Jesus and being all that God has ordained them to be to the end that we be as cornerstones polished after the palace.

DOZI was birthed in the place of a certain delay I experienced and via service, I had to find the "more" that's in me even as I fellowshipped with God, then it began to open up. Nothing spectacular at inception, just obedience to the voice I heard in response to a period of much burdens and concerns emanating from circumstances many ladies were trapped in which was a pain in my heart.

As I journeyed it became clearer and still getting clear. Funny enough, I've never heard the name anywhere before as at the time of setting out (until a year later), it was divinely received.

Mentorship

Deborah's Crib (DC) is the mentorship arm of Daughters of Zion Int'l, under the covering of Pst Faithtestimony A. This extension came up in the year 2018 and is focused on training, watching over and raising young ladies into Fulfilment of their God given destiny. Equipping and empowering a breed of women for Kingdom exploits. This involves a great deal of commitment and accountability.
