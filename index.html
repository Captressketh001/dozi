More about Daughters of Zion International

DOZI is an assembly of God’s daughters, Christ’s bride and the redeemed. Here, tribe, location, or class is no barrier. We are knitted together by Christ’s love.

We’re a non-denominational Christian ministry borne out in response to God's voice, inspiration of the HOLY SPIRIT and passion for the female generation with the burden to see ladies fired up, awakened to purpose and destiny, live up to their rightful position in CHRIST JESUS and be the best God has ordained them to be. DOZI is a place of equipping, building and training destinies; reconciling daughters (young and old alike) back to their heritage in God.

Meet the Convener

Faithtestimony O. Akenroye is a daughter of Zion, prophetic teacher of God's word, an ordained pastor, a minstrel, counsellor, prolific writer, and mentor to several young ladies. She is on a rescue mission to see female folks fired up and become all that God has destined or ordained them to be, as they fully explore their God-given potentials and live up to their rightful places in Christ Jesus. This is aimed at getting our daughters as corner stones polished after the similitude of a palace.She has been doing this over time via her writings and ministrations on various online and on-site platforms. She is committed to reinforcing accurate kingdom womanhood. She serves as the spiritual midwife @ Deborah's Crib, a mentoring hub where she nurtures, trains and watches over a community of ladies into fulfilment of their God’s given destinies, through close monitoring and accountability system.

She is also the Lead Mentor at Deborah's Crib Mentoring School, a transformation centre where women are groomed to recognize and harness their divine giftings for kingdom advancement, equipped and empowered for kingdom exploits, as well as taught to grow in their walk with God. She is the founder of Daughters of Zion Int’l, a non-denominational ministry with the vision of rescuing daughters from Babylon to Zion, raising women of relevance after God’s own Heart that will uphold zion's standards, reconciling daughters (young and old alike) to their heritage in God and reaching out to the female generation. She holds the ‘Unfading Beauty Series’ on this platform, every Wednesday. She also convenes several other life-changing meetings through this platform. Some of them include; the School of prayers, Campus Ladies Prayer Conference, School of Purpose, Sisters’ Time Out, Operation Rescue Them and many others that keep blessing lives over the years.

She is an author of life-changing books such as Jewel of Inestimable Value, Damsel Arise and The Woman after God's heart among others.

DOZI Programmes

Unfading Beauty

A weekly teaching session targeted at raising women after God's heart. Holds every Wednesday on our WhatsApp platforms

Operation Rescue Them (ORT)

An annual outreach to female students in secondary schools, rescuing them from sin, sexual abuse and satanic oppression.

Zionite Pray's

A monthly prayer meeting of daughters of Zion and female intercessors on various campuses interceding for the land, the campus and the nation through prayers and supplication.

Sister's Time Out

An annual physical convergence of ladies for a great time of impartation at His feet in word and prayers. Holds July every year, also scheduled to celebrate our anniversary.

Moment With the King

A 30mins intervention prayer meeting. Holds last day of the month, live on our Facebook page.
